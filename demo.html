üéØ Suggested Demo Scenarios

1. Scale Practice with Drone

1. Click the "Drone: A (440Hz)" preset

2. Click Play to hear a continuous A tone

3. Practice A major or A minor scales against this drone

2. Chord Progression Practice

1. Click the "C Major I-vi-IV-V" preset

2. Adjust tempo to your comfort level (try 80-120 BPM)

3. Enable Loop Mode and click Play

4. Practice playing along with the chords

3. Custom Progression

1. In the text area, enter: D | A | G | D

2. Click "Parse Chords"

3. Set tempo to 100 BPM

4. Try both tuning modes to hear the difference

4. Blues Practice

1. Click the "12-Bar Blues in A" preset

2. Enable Metronome Click for rhythm reference

3. Practice improvising over the 12-bar form

5. Build Custom Chords

1. Scroll down to the Piano Keyboard section

2. Click on piano keys to select notes (try C, E, G for a C major chord)

3. Click "Play Selected" to hear your chord

4. Click "Add to Progression" to add it to your sequence

6. Interactive Chord Preview

1. Load any preset or create a custom progression

2. Click on any chord in the "Current Progression" section

3. Hear the chord instantly without starting full playback
